How Jack earned his First $100 in Snack

Hi, it's Jack again!

Where did we leave off last time? Oh, yes, exactly how I was looking for passive income options and found out about Snack, which turned out to be many times more effective than currency trading. And now I'll tell you more about how I earned my first $100 in the app!

To be honest, at first I thought that everything was somehow suspiciously simple. When I started to understand how it works in detail, I found out that traffic sharing is not the only way to earn in Snack. You can get more by inviting friends to sign up and playing a daily win-win cookie lottery. Well. Of course, first of all, I decided to test the main option — earning on traffic sharing!

I still have a fairly large amount of unused traffic at the end of a month: I spend a lot of time at work in a car service where we have Wi-Fi, so I mostly use it on my smartphone. At first, I set up a small amount of traffic to share and launched the app in the background. I even forgot about it! And after a while, I checked the balance and saw several dollars there! I was very surprised, because I literally did nothing to earn them!

Well... It was super attractive! I decided to try extra ways to get money in Snac

I told five of my friends about the app, they got interested and also started earning there, and I get 10% of their daily income for the invitation! And my wife and brother also now use Snack and invite their friends to join. Meanwhile, in the cookie lottery you can easily win several hundred coins. Once I won 1000!

Since then, our family budget has been growing. And somehow, playing my favorite mobile game on the way home, I realized that I can double my earnings in Snack! I just need to connect a second device to my account. We use our family laptop much less than smartphones, so sometimes there is more traffic left than we use in a month. And now, there are two active devices in my account that help me earn even more every day!

Soon, I saved up my first $100 only thanks to Snack. My happiness was endless! My wife and I started planning a sea journey, which we had been dreaming about for a long time, but could not save up enough money. Such plans no longer seem impossible — especially considering that my wife has also become an active Snack user.

And just like that… Yesterday we bought plane tickets and this summer we are off to the sea for two weeks! It's incredible! Snack is really a thing and now this is my favorite app! We're already making up a wishlist of things we'll buy with money earned there!
