What is Snack?
Snack allows users to get money for their unused Internet traffic, and simultaneously facilitate large companies' inner research, making it more accurate and efficient. The service guarantees a reward for every gigabyte of user's traffic and provides access to it for trusted partners.
On which platforms does Snack work?
At the moment, Snack is available on Windows and Android devices.
In which countries is Snack available?
Snack is available worldwide.
How does Snack work?
Snack uses you untapped traffic to help big tech companies to conduct inner research and make the global Internet a better place. You decide what amount of traffic you can share with the app, while Snack, in turn, share it with its trusted partners to facilitate inner research.
Is it safe to use Snack?
Yes. Snack does not collect user data, including browser search history or activity records. Each user remains completely anonymous.
How to get started?
  • -Download Snack to your device
  • -Open the app
  • -Sign up with your email and password to get access to all features
  • -Verify your email
  • -Connect your device and set the amount of traffic you're ready to share with the app
How the shared traffic will be used?
Your traffic will be used by large companies to conduct digital research. For example, to check online ads display, analyze competitors, monitor brand, perform web scrapping, compare prices for goods and services.
How can I earn more in Snack?
  • There are two ways to increase your income in Snack:
  • -Scale up the volume of traffic you share
  • -Invite friends to join Snack and get benefits
Why is my Snack account not verified?
Upon the registration in Snack, we will send you an email with a confirmation link. Click on it to confirm your email. If you haven't received the email, request a resend.
How to add an extra device to your Snack account?
To add a device to your account, download and install the Snack app to the device that you're going to use and log in to your account there.
How many devices can I connect to my Snack account?
You can connect up to 10 separate devices
How to change the language settings?
The default app language corresponds to the one of your browser. On Android devices, the app language settings copy those of the device. You can change the app language by clicking on the corresponding button on the website.
How to increase the volume of traffic I share?
You can scale up the volume of traffic you are ready to share in your account's dashboard.
How to stop sharing traffic?
You can stop sharing traffic by activating this option in your account settings.
How to delete my Snack account?
To delete your account, select the relevant option in your Snack account settings.
What is Snack internal currency (Snack coin, SC) exchange rate?
1000 SC = 1$
How my earnings from traffic sharing are calculated?
Your earnings depend on the volume of traffic you share with the app, your location and the demand for traffic at a point in time. On average, you get 20 cents (200 Snack coins) for 1 Gb of traffic.
What is the minimum balance threshold to withdraw money?
Yes. 10$ is the minimum threshold.
What payout options are available in Snack?
You can withdraw funds using Wise money transfer service.
How to make a payout?
  • Make sure that you've reached a 10$ threshold on your inner account balance
  • -Press the payout button on your dashboard
  • -Follow the instructions on the payout process via Wise service. Note that you can withdraw only the full sum accumulated on your internal Snack balance
  • -And now relax and wait a little til the money gets wired to your account - sometimes it could take some time
Are there any peculiarities of the app functioning on different platforms?
Some Android devices (most often Huawei and Xiaomi) may block background app functioning. To continue using the service, disable activity monitoring for the Snack app in the device's settings.
