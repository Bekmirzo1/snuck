How I earn while doing literally nothing?

Hey! If you don't know me yet, my name is Jack and I'm a car mechanic

I recently got married, and this got me thinking seriously about what's going on with my income and free time that I want to spend with my beloved wife.

I tried currency trading but, you know, it's risky… You'll never know. Basically, there were no tangible outputs, I just wasted time.

Then I started diving into investments. But when you try to figure it out, your head is just spinning! After all, stocks' prices are constantly rising and falling: there are so many risks again, and it's almost impossible to assess them and calculate income without adequate preparation! Plus, you need to invest some of your savings, and does a newly-formed family have considerable savings? Moreover, it's really scary to lose all hard-earned savings just because of some kind of sudden economic collapse. With an average yield of 7%, the initial deposit will be repelled in more than 10 years if everything goes according to plan. There are also brokers who know this stuff, but you need to pay them for services, which again somehow does not really look like an easy passive income.

But then I heard about Snack

I did not immediately believe that it was possible to get stable passive income without risks and investments. So I started getting into it in more detail. It turns out that you share your traffic and get paid for that. That's all! No risks, investments, stress, uncertainty.

I downloaded Snack and was surprised that it really works! I realized it in the first week of using the app. You press the button and everything is under way. Moreover, there is no need to constantly monitor things, sell and buy stocks — I just withdraw funds when I need them. You start earning exactly upon pressing the «start sharing traffic» button, there are no risks of a currency collapse or a drop in the value of shares — just a dream! It comes out about $5-7 per month from one device, but this is not the limit.

We already installed Snack on my wife's smartphone and our laptop. The more devices are active, the more profit you get! I figured out that income from 3 devices cover payments for gasoline or morning coffee on the way. I mean that we can save this money for our future home or college fees of our children! By the way, even if I were trading stocks and everything would be fine, the income from Snack would be comparable to those initial $1000 I had to invest in stocks to start receiving dividends. But here you definitely won't go broke and lose your money.

Here we are! Now I'm finishing my free coffee and going to share my discovery with a colleague, he has also been looking for ways to earn effortlessly!
